Framework for
local communities
Ole Kirk’s Fond has, over the years, supported the establishment and refurbishment of the physical frameworks for local communities for children, youths, and their families in Billund Municipality.
For example, we have supported:
A new outdoor area in the town of Vorbasse which connects the town’s leisure centre and school. Ole Kirk’s Fond has awarded: DKK 3.0 million.
Multi-functional rooms and outdoor area for the local school in the town of Hejnsvig, which will be re-established and converted into a new meeting place for the town – with a school, a library, sports centre, a community centre, and much more. Ole Kirk’s Fond has awarded: DKK 3.0 million to the project.
A new building for the private independent school and kindergarten in the town of Filskov, which can form the framework for creative and craft-related subjects. A house for pupils and the townspeople. Ole Kirk’s Fond has awarded: DKK 5.0 million.
A thorough renovation of Billund’s swimming baths, and a new area for the cafeteria and foyer. Ole Kirk’s Fond has awarded: DKK 16.0 million.
