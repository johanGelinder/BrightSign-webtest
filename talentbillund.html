Facts
’Talent Billund’ offers children and youths in Billund Municipality the possibility to develop and improve their skills in their chosen sport, music, painting, or dance activities – in addition to the training and community in their own clubs.
They can combine their ordinary schooling with professional training and instruction to a high level and, at the same time, be a part of a community across various cultural disciplines and sports branches.
More than one hundred local children and youths are attached to this talent project – and it gives everyone a possibility to participate in a community with others who also cultivate their free time interests to a high level.
