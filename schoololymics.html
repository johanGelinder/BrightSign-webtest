The School Olympics is an Olympic Games for children. The aim of the event is to create unity and well-being for schoolchildren via sporting activities – and with a focus on the inclusion of special needs children.
Here, school classes from all over Denmark compete against each other in various disciplines.
The games start with local qualification rounds and culminate in an Olympic Final that is held over several days. Billund Municipality will host the finals until 2024 in the ‘Capital of Children’, Billund.
